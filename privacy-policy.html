Your Privacy with PerfectPlate

Privacy Policy for PerfectPlate

Last Updated: January 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PerfectPlate. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website or mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data you provide directly, such as your name, email address, dietary preferences, allergies, favorite foods, and payment details for subscriptions. Additionally, we automatically collect usage data, including your interaction with meal recommendations, device information, and app usage patterns. If you voluntarily provide dietary and health details for personalization, we use this information with your explicit consent. Cookies and similar technologies help us track preferences and improve your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide personalized meal recommendations tailored to your dietary goals, improve the app’s functionality, process subscription payments, communicate updates or support information, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. Your information may be shared with third-party providers for payment processing and app hosting. We may also share data with legal authorities when required or if necessary to protect our rights. Content shared through the app, such as meal suggestions or reviews, will only be shared with other users if you opt to use the sharing feature.

5. Data Security

Your data is secured using industry-standard encryption. While we strive to protect your information, no transmission or storage method is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information through the app’s settings. For additional assistance, you can email us at perfectplateai@gmail.com.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will include a new “Last Updated” date and will be accessible within the app.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, contact us at perfectplateai@gmail.com.
