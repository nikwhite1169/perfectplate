PerfectPlate FAQ

General Questions

1. What is PerfectPlate?
PerfectPlate is a personalized dining assistant app that helps users find and customize dishes at restaurants based on their diets, allergies, and goals such as weight loss or increasing protein intake.

2. How does PerfectPlate work?
Users input their dietary preferences, allergies, and goals into the app. PerfectPlate then recommends menu items at restaurants and provides detailed instructions to customize the dish to meet the user’s needs, including nutritional information.

3. Where can I download PerfectPlate?
PerfectPlate is available now for download on both iOS and Android.

4. How does PerfectPlate use AI?
PerfectPlate uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze menus and recommend dishes tailored to your preferences and goals. However, as with any AI, it’s not always perfect and may occasionally make mistakes. We recommend double-checking recommendations, especially if you have serious allergies or specific dietary concerns.

5. Are new features planned for PerfectPlate?
Yes! We are constantly innovating and working on exciting new features to enhance your experience. Stay tuned for updates that will make PerfectPlate even more powerful and versatile.

Features

6. Does PerfectPlate consider specific dietary goals?
Yes! Whether you're looking to lose weight, increase protein intake, or meet other dietary goals, PerfectPlate adjusts its recommendations accordingly.

7. Can the app handle multiple allergies or dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. You can input as many allergies and dietary restrictions as needed, and PerfectPlate will only recommend dishes that are safe for you. However, we recommend double-checking all recommendations to ensure your needs are met.

8. Does PerfectPlate provide detailed nutritional information?
Yes, the app calculates and displays macros (nutritional data) for each recommended dish.

9. Can I input my favorite dish and customize it?
Yes! You can input any dish you love, and PerfectPlate will suggest modifications to make it align with your dietary needs and goals. The app also provides clear instructions for communicating these customizations to the restaurant staff.

Restaurants

10. How does PerfectPlate choose restaurants?
The app pulls restaurant options from Google Maps, so you have access to any restaurant listed in your area.

11. Can I use PerfectPlate while traveling?
Yes, as long as there are restaurants listed on Google Maps in your location, PerfectPlate can provide recommendations.
